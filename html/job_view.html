Medical Registrars - Trainee Position

Darwin, All Darwin

$122,000 - $152,565 Annual Salary

Permanent

  • • Medical Trainees Registrars - BPT/Advanced Training - Level 3 Hospital


  • • Turquoise oceans/breathtaking sunsets, Tropical savanna climate


  • • Attractive package - Career progression - Excellent opportunity


The Role: We are seeking Trainee Medical Registrars to join a dedicated team, working in a level 3, Teaching Hospital. This is
an attractive opportunity due to the complexity of the medical illnesses as well as the challenging psychosocial
circumstances.

The rich clinical experience at Hospital has resulted in a strong link between the Division of Medicine and clinicians
from Oxford, UK. There are regular visiting specialists from UK who come for the unique tropical clinical
experience. There is the possibility of our clinicians going to the UK also. mutually beneficial manner

The Hospital:

The Hospital has 345 beds servicing a population of over 140,000 it has a diverse population including urban,
regional and remote centres.

This is the main tertiary referral centre for Northern Australia with the capacity to manage a wide range of clinical
conditions. In the clinical setting the Northern Territory offers some of the most unique opportunities and clinical
presentations not seen in other parts of Australia.

You will enjoy a tropical savanna climate, typically sits at 25-33°C all year round. Unlike the rest of Australia, which
experiences four distinct seasons, you will only experience 2 here, The Dry and The Wet.

A City that is nestled amongst some of the most stunning and untouched parts of Australia with turquoise oceans
and breathtaking sunsets, powerful waterfalls and pristine hot springs, monsoonal rainforest pockets and lily pad
dotted billabongs.

The landscape varies greatly, from inner-city apartment living through to sprawling acreage properties. Homes are
typically designed to celebrate the outdoors including large entertaining areas, tropical gardens and swimming
pools.

Mark Steyn

Director & Chief Executive Officer

Mark is Hudson’s Managing Director and CEO and is responsible for guiding the company’s growth strategy. Over
ten years Mark has established Hudson as a leading provider of talent solutions in the Asia Pacific region and
recently led the management buy-out of its specialist recruitment and talent management business. Hudson is
now the largest privately-owned talent solutions company in Asia Pacific.

In a career spanning over 28 years, Mark has lived in Australia, Canada, England, Scotland, USA, Thailand and
Hong Kong.Mark started his career with Andersen Consulting and after four years joined Strategy& (previously Booz Allen &
Hamilton) where he spent eight years consulting to blue chip companies around the world. Mark moved into the
corporate world in 2001, joining the NAB as a Group Executive in the Office of the CEO. He went on to become the
General Manager of Operations for Australia and New Zealand before relocating to Hong Kong where he headed
the NAB’s Retail & Private Banking for Asia.

Prior to joining Hudson, Mark spent two years establishing Reveal Group, a global business consulting firm
specializing in Operations and Technology.

Mark has a Bachelor of Business and Bachelor of Applied Science both from Monash University, Victoria (1988).
