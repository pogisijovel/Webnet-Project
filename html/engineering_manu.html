Engineering and manufacturing recruitment

A strong foundation of industry knowledge at your service

Our specialist engineering and manufacturing team is responsible for permanent, interim, and contract recruitment solutions to industrial clients across the UK. We use our detailed market knowledge and in-depth understanding of automotive, aerospace, capital equipment and general manufacturing to connect clients and candidates with like-minded aspirations and processes.

With over 30 years of combined experience, our consultants recruit for an extensive range of roles from senior leadership within manufacturing operations, engineering and project management, through to out-and-out technical specialists within design, development, quality and engineering.
